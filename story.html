OUR STORY

╲╱

How We Got Started

Students and Seniors United was founded by Ramana Kolady, who always had a passion for helping the elderly, as well as medicine. From a young age, he has been passionate about helping senior citizens in any way possible to make them feel needed, wanted, and happy in their community, so he started Students and Seniors United to fulfill this exact purpose! Initially, the organization only had 2 people...

╲╱

Hurdles

When Ramana first had the idea for Students and Seniors United, he intended for it to be a school club. However, his high school rejected the idea, and Ramana decided to brand it into a Community Organization. Even after this development, we faced many hurdles, including members leaving, struggles to identify colleagues who were the most committed, and a lack of funding, but we ultimately pushed through, thanks to good leadership, and even better people.

╲╱

Where We Are Now.

We are now an organization with 40+ active members and are continuing to expand. With these dedicated members who have a great passion for helping senior citizens, we will continue to strive for our mission of bringing the community together by helping and learning from our senior citizens. We plan to start holding events and providing entertainment for the seniors soon!
