RESEARCH

Students and Seniors United obtains research on issues affecting senior citizens from seniors at different senior centers. We assign groups to research specific topics such as social isolation, exercise among seniors and more. Each group asks questions to senior citizens to understand the issue from their perspective. After doing so, these students present their findings back to the group.

INNOVATION

Students and Seniors United uses its research to develop innovative solutions to issues affecting senior citizens. Our team consists of a diverse group of future engineers, programmers, healthcare professionals, and more. Currently, we are working with Priya Living.
